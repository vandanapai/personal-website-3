Boost Healthcare Consulting

Boost’s website was stuck in 1998, and they were concerned with the offhand comments they were receiving from clients about it. They needed a major refresh in order to demonstrate to clients their professionalism.

Role

Freelance web designer and developer

Tools Used

HTML5, SCSS, Javascript/jQuery

Process

Conducting User Research

Getting an understanding of the people at Boost was my first task. It was important to me to understand the vibe of the team, what everyone wanted the website to achieve, and the main message they wanted to get across. We went through many thought questions like “How would you define Boost?”, “How do you imagine people to use your website?” and “How do you want users to feel upon first visiting your website?”.
